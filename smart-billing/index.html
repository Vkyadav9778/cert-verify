What Smart Billing does

📷

Scan or speak to bill

Scan a barcode or just say the item name — it's added to the bill instantly.

💬

Send bills on WhatsApp

One tap sends a formatted bill straight to the customer's WhatsApp.

📦

Manage your stock

Add, edit, and remove products — prices, units, and categories, all in your control.

📶

Works fully offline

Every feature works with zero internet — billing never has to wait for signal.

💾

Automatic local backup

Your data is backed up on your own phone automatically, after every change.

☁️

Optional Drive backup

Connect your own Google Drive for an extra safety copy — private to your account only.
